Chicken Curry Recipe

Description

Chicken Curry is a great all round choice with plenty of nutritional value. There is also the added benefit of being able to re-heat leftovers for the next days lunch.

It is not only quick and easy to prepare but also only requires a small amount of ingredients that are widely available.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsb of vegetable oil
  • 2 tsb of mango chutney
  • 2 tsb of curry powder
  • 1 tsb of worchester sauce
  • 2 tsb tomato sauce
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 2 chicken breasts, chopped into small pieces
  • 100g coconut milk
  • A small amount of chopped bazil
  • 30g ground almonds
  • 2 slices of brown bread and served with cooked bens original rice,(other brands are available!)

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan over a medium heat. Add a generous pinch of salt and fry for around 7-10 mins.
  2. Then chop the chicken into chunky 2cm pieces, add to the pan.Now add the mango chutney, curry powder,worchester sauce,tomato sauce,chicken stock,coconut milk,bazil and almonds
  3. Fry for 7 mins before stirring through the additives mixture, along with 250ml water. Bring to the boil, lower to a simmer and cook on a gentle heat uncovered for 25-30 mins or until rich and slightly reduced.
  4. Stir though the curry mixture, season once more with another pinch of salt and serve with rice.
