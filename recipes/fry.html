Classic Fried Food

Description

This should be called a heart attack on a plate! While certainly most dietitians wouldn't reccomend this dish it is perfectly fine in moderation and is guarenteed to taste nice

Ingredients

  • 3 beef saussages from your local butcher or butchers in quality supermarket
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2 Free range eggs or your local store best quality variety
  • 4 slices of brown bread either buttered or toasted then buttered

Steps

  1. Add your local heart attack oil variety to a frying pan.Pre-heat the frying pan for 3/5 minutes.
  2. When you can hear or see the oil crackiling get ready to add most of the goods.Now add the saussages and bacon
  3. Turn over regularly to ensure an even cook and cook in total for 10-15 minutes
  4. Add the eggs and cook to your desired time and consistency
  5. Then serve with brown bread and beverage on your choice, enjoy!
