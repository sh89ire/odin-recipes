Fried Chicken

Description

Who dosen't like fried chicken? also instead of everything else being said to taste just like chicken this actallly does because it is! The fact you have crispy batter there along with it being fried which always makes everything taste better you won't be disappointed!

Ingredients

  • 4 whisked eggs(free range if possible)
  • 100 ml of filtered water
  • 1 tsb worchester sauce
  • 100g coconut milk
  • 100g all round flower
  • 2 tsb salt
  • 1 tsb honey
  • 1 tsb sugar
  • 2 Chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Whisk 4 eggs until correct egg wash consistency
  2. Add water,worchester sauce,coconut milk,honey,salt and sugar to egg wash and whisk again
  3. Cut chicken into 8 pieces,then dip into egg wash
  4. Place into flower and coat evenly,then dip back into egg wash and then into the flower for a final time
  5. Heat oil in pan for 5 mins, then place a small amount of batter to check it is frying. If it sizzles then add the chicken pieces
  6. Turn over regularly to ensure an even cook and overall cooking time should be 10-12 mins
  7. Place in plate covered with paper towel, allow to sit for 2 mins then serve
